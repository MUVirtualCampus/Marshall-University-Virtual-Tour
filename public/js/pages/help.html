Marshall University Virtual Tour Help Guide

Getting Started



Upon startup, you will be directed to the dashboard page (referenced in the image above) where you can click the 'Let's Get Started' button (circled in red), to begin your tour.



Basic Navigation



Looking at the floor, you will notice white arrows pointing in a variety of directions. Upon clicking an arrow, the panorama will advance you to the next panorama in the direction of the arrow.



Navigating The Panorama



Within the panorama, you have a variety of features to manipulate the image and get the most out of your touring experience. Using your mouse wheel, you can zoom in and out on the image, centered on your mouse pointer. You can also zoom by using the '+' and '-' buttons on the bottom right hand corner of the screen.

By clicking on the image and dragging your mouse, you can rotate the image 360Â° horizontally as well as vertically. You can even see the ceiling and the floor!

Current location data is displayed within the black box in the top left corner of the screen. Within this box, you can tell the building you are in and the floor you are on.



Navigating The Menu



Clicking on the 'Hamburger Stack' (circled in red) in the top left will open the 'Navigation' menu. Within this menu you can perform a variety of features related to quickly navigating the tour.

Clicking on the dropdown menu directly under the 'Navigation' heading will allow you to select the building, or area, you wish to tour.

Clicking on the dropdown menu directly under the building picker dropdown, will allow you to change your floor within the building you are already in.



Quick Jump



The last feature of the tour is related to the 'Quick Jump' feature. To activate the feature, click on the 'Quick Jump' button (circled in red).



Quick Jump



Clicking on the 'Quick Jump' button will bring up the 'Quick Jump' menu, which displays places of interest in your current building. After selecting a place of interest, you will be transported to that place and corresponding information, about that room, will be displayed in the 'Information' container below the 'Quick Jump' button..
